## 💰 This Bot Answers:

**Sample conversations built-in:**

**Q:** "What's your pricing?"  
**A:** Provides detailed pricing for discovery call ($0), 1-on-1 ($200/session), 3-month package ($2,400), 6-month package ($4,200)

**Q:** "How do I book a call?"  
**A:** Provides Calendly link and email

**Q:** "Do you offer career coaching?"  
**A:** Yes, explains specialties (career transitions, life purpose, work-life balance, etc.)

**Q:** "What's included in the 3-month package?"  
**A:** Lists everything: 12 sessions, email support, resources, etc.

**Q:** "How long are sessions?"  
**A:** 60 minutes via Zoom, flexible scheduling

**Edge cases handled:**
- Medical advice → Declines properly
- Crisis situations → Provides 988 hotline
- Legal/financial → Refers to professionals
- Angry/upset → Offers human handoff
